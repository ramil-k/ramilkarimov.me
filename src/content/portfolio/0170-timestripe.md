# Timestripe

Part-time contract frontend developer (Mar 2025 – present) across two Timestripe products.

**Timestripe Magazine** is an editorial publication and content hub — 95+ articles on productivity, goal-setting, focus, and team collaboration, plus a Learning Center and product reviews. Built on SvelteKit 5 with Svelte 5 runes, static output, and a custom design system (IDS). I built and maintain the publication platform: feed generation (JSON/XML/Atom/Sitemap), server-side image optimization, tag-based content organization, and the core rendering engine.

**Timestripe App** is a goal-tracking and productivity platform (web, iOS, Android, Chrome extension) built in React 18 with a custom platform abstraction layer. There I focused on the Inbox feature (desktop and mobile), mobile dashboard widgets, and fixed drag-and-drop filtering in the collaboration board.

**Stack:** SvelteKit 5, Svelte 5, React 18, TypeScript, Vite, PostCSS, React Beautiful DND, Workbox (PWA), Biome, Sentry
