Telegram Mini App for running a pet photo contest within a Telegram channel community. Users submit photos of their pets via the Mini App interface, and admins can review, approve, and publish submissions directly to the channel.

Built with SvelteKit 2 and Svelte 5 (runes), featuring secure Telegram authentication via HMAC-SHA256 initData validation, channel subscription verification, session-based browser login via Telegram Login Widget, and native MainButton/BackButton integration for seamless Telegram UX. The app includes a full admin panel with submission moderation, user management, and one-click publication to the Telegram channel.
